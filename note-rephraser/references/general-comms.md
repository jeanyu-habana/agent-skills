# General Communication Guidelines

Before drafting, gather key information:
1. Identify who will receive this message
2. Determine what you're trying to accomplish
3. Establish an appropriate tone level
4. Note any special formatting needs

## Writing Principles
- Keep language straightforward and brief
- Favor active voice constructions
- Lead with your most critical point
- Incorporate relevant URLs and citations
- Maintain consistency with your organization's existing communication patterns

The framework emphasizes front-loading importance and maintaining clarity across your audience segments.
